12. Spell Checking

Goal: Spot typos instantly during draft editing.

How it works: Real-time checks underline spelling errors with red squiggles. Right-clicking a word opens a menu with spelling alternatives, an option to ignore the word for the session, or add the word permanently to your custom dictionary.
